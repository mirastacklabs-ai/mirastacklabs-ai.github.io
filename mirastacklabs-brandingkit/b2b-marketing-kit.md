# MIRASTACK LABS B2B Marketing Kit

## 1. Strategic narrative

### Market problem

Enterprise platform teams in regulated sectors face fragmented tooling, weak root-cause explainability, and cloud-driven control loss.

### MIRASTACK narrative

MIRASTACK unifies sovereign platform operations with explainable AI intelligence, giving enterprise operators both speed and governance in air-gapped contexts.

### Outcome narrative

From detection to remediation, teams move faster with auditable confidence across hardware, network, runtime, and business service layers.

## 2. Core campaign themes

1. Sovereign Platform Engineering
2. Explainable Incident Intelligence
3. Air-Gapped AI Operations for Regulated Environments
4. From Telemetry Noise to Operator Decisions

## 3. Segment message matrix

### For CTO/CIO

- Message: Reduce strategic dependency risk while modernizing AI operations.
- Value: Sovereign control, architecture standardization, reduced cloud lock-in.
- CTA: Book an executive architecture briefing.

### For CISO/Compliance

- Message: Increase governance and auditability of AI-assisted operations.
- Value: Human-in-the-loop controls, explainable RCA, compliance-ready workflow.
- CTA: Request governance and controls walkthrough.

### For VP Platform Engineering

- Message: Correlate full-stack signals and remediate incidents faster.
- Value: Shorter MTTI and MTTR, better release confidence, operational clarity.
- CTA: Run a technical proof sprint.

### For SRE/DevSecOps leaders

- Message: Bring observability and AI reasoning into daily incident workflow.
- Value: Better triage quality, reduced alert fatigue, faster root-cause validation.
- CTA: Schedule a product deep dive.

## 5. Launch campaign blueprint (90 days)

### Month 1: Authority setup

- Publish flagship narrative page and two thought-leadership posts
- Release one technical explainer for platform teams
- Enable LinkedIn leadership narrative from founder and product leaders

### Month 2: Demand activation

- Run one webinar and one roundtable for enterprise architecture leaders
- Publish two technical case-pattern articles
- Start targeted outreach for design partners and lighthouse accounts

### Month 3: Pipeline conversion

- Launch guided architecture assessment offer
- Run account-based follow-ups with role-specific messaging
- Publish one implementation checklist and one governance brief
